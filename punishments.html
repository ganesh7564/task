What is the Punishment for Being Late?

If you are late for more than 5 minutes, you will have to perform one of these tasks:

  • sing a song

If you are late for more than 10 minutes, you will have to perform one of these tasks:

  • dance on a song

If you are late for more than 15 minutes, you will have to perform one of these tasks:

  • treat for the class

If you are late for more than 20 minutes, you will have to perform one of these tasks:

  • give your phone to teacher and take it at 4:40 pm

If you are late for more than 25 minutes, you will have to perform one of these tasks:

  • no attendence for this lecture and next lecture

If you are late for more than 30 minutes, you will have to perform one of these tasks:

  • clean the whole class

Warning

If you are consistently late, you will be suspended for 1 week .
